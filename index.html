About The Event

EESA Team is presenting you the most exciting event "code-o-fiesta" Edition II. The event is a reflection of mock placements comprehending three rounds viz. Aptitude Test, Surprise round and HR Interview. Along with a well structured Test and stirring Surprise Round, the HR Interview has a professional touch too. an online event that involves people interacting in a virtual environment on the web, rather than meeting in a physical location. Virtual events are typically multi-session online events that often feature webinars and webcasts. They are highly interactive, often aiming to create as similar an experience as possible to their physical counterparts
